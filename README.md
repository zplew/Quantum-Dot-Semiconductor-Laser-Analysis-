# Quantum-Dot-Semiconductor-Laser-Analysis-
Modeling a Semiconductor Quantum Dot Laser by simulating confined electron and hole states in a three-dimensional finite potential well. Computes:
Electron and Hole Energies
Wavefunctions and Probability Densities (3D, 1-D Slices, 2-D Slices)
Overlap Integrals
State-Matching Analysis
Photon Emission Energies and Wavelengths
Emission Spectra and Intensity

The model incorporates key physical effects including:
Quantum Confinement (particle-in-a-box)
Finite Potential Barrier Penetration
Stark Effect (external electric field)
Temperature-Dependent Band Gap
Exciton Binding Energy
